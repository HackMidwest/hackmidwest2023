Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between food surplus and scarcity. Every day, tons of edible food is thrown away. At the same time, countless individuals go to bed hungry. We aim to connect businesses with excess food to those in need, creating a win-win situation for our community and the environment.

Our Outreach

  • Partnered with 10 local bakeries to distribute unsold goods
  • Organized monthly community dinners feeding over 500 homeless individuals
  • Launched a mobile app connecting donors with local shelters in real-time

Our Vision

A world where no edible food goes to waste, and every individual has access to nutritious meals. By leveraging technology and fostering community partnerships, we envision a future where food redistribution becomes the norm, not the exception.

Where We're Going

  • Expand our network to cover 5 more cities by 2025
  • Launch a training program to educate businesses about food wastage
  • Collaborate with schools to introduce food redistribution programs
